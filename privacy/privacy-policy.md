# Privacy Policy for Arthropod Invasion

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Arthropod Invasion does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The game stores settings and gameplay information, such as the high score, locally on the player's device. This information is used only to provide game functionality and is not transmitted to the developer or third parties.

The game does not contain advertising, analytics, user accounts, location tracking, or in-app purchases. It does not request access to contacts, photos, the camera, the microphone, or location.

If a future version changes these practices, this policy will be updated before that version is released.

## Children's privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other players.
